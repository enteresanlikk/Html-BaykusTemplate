about the owls?

Nightlife is the time for hiding and sleeping for many creatures, while for some it is time for hunting. However, there is a big disadvantage in night fishing; dark. For this reason, it is a must for a night hunter. The owls, who are the best of night hunters, are the assets that God has created with these special equipment.


The hearing system of owls is quite superior to many other living things. His ears are found behind his eyes, near his head. The threshold of a bird's sensation is not different from that of a human. But since owls are more sensitive to voices at certain frequencies, they hear even the slightest voices of leaves or the prey under the bushes.
